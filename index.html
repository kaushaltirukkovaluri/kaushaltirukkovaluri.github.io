Kaushal Tirukkovaluri

Designer and Photographer

My passion for photography dates back to my childhood when I started experimenting with the cameras that used to have negative reels and later with cameras on smartphones until I recently got coupled to a Canon60d. The beautiful minute details I find in aspects of nature urges me to take photographs and store them as a memory, as a gift for myself. I find my happiness in hanging around with my Canon60d and frame the awesome moments I find in my life. Design and post processing of my photos pulls me more into tinkering with cameras and studying the art of photography. My secret of success for taking a beautiful yet awesome photograph is peace of mind and trying for innovation.
